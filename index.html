The Annual Art Portfolio Exposition 2020 - 2021

The Annual Art Exposition

The Art students of Classes IX to XII are proud to present the 8th Annual Art Portfolio Exposition 2020-21. The online gallery showcases the emergence of a deeper engagement and developing faculties of these young artists through their course work under the curriculum prescribed by the ICSE and ISC Boards. This exposition also provides a good idea about the contents of the ICSE and ISC Art syllabi.

A major component of their learning experiences, this annual event provides the budding artists a platform to present their work before the entire Shikshantar community, see and appreciate their peers’ work and assess their own growth and achievements. Over the past years, the extraordinary talent of the students has inspired many a young aspirant and won plenty of accolades from the visitors, young and old.

This year, for the first time ever, the Exposition is an online experience, for which we owe our thanks to a team of students of the School IT Committee and the Visual Arts Committee, who have worked very hard behind the scenes to design and execute this website and make it accessible to us from the safety of our homes.

We invite you to go through the student portfolios, witness their journey and appreciate their hard work. We hope you enjoy it as much as the students enjoyed creating these works!
